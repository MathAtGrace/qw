Exercise 14.

Fifty juniors and fifty seniors at a local high school were surveyed to find out how many hours per week they spend studying. The side-by-side boxplots the weekly study times for those high school juniors and seniors.
  1. Estimate the 25th, 50th, and 75th percentiles for weekly study time for high school juniors and seniors.
  2. Olivia studies the maximum number of weekly study hours for a junior. Lucy studies the first quartile weekly study time for a senior. Who studies more, and by how many hours?
  3. What percentage of juniors study between the minimum and median weekly study times for seniors?
  4. What percentage of seniors study more than the third quartile weekly study time for juniors?
