Activity 5.2.5.

  1. A population grows by 4.5% per year. What is its annual growth factor?
  2. In 2018, the average annual cost of attending a public college was $\(21{,}370\text{,}\) and costs were climbing by 6.5% per year. Write a formula for \(C(t)\text{,}\) the cost of one year of college \(t\) years after 2018.
  3. Compute values of \(C(t)\) when \(t = 0, 5, 10, 15, 20, 25\text{.}\)
  4. Sketch a graph of \(C(t)\) that includes all of your answers above.
  5. If the percent growth rate continues to remain steady, how much will a year of college cost in 2040?
  6. Describe carefully the meanings of \(b\) and \(r\) in the formulas for exponential growth.
