The backyard is 130*115 + 10*55 = 15500 square feet.
\begin{equation*} 15500\text{ sq ft} \cdot \frac{1\text{ pound}}{250\text{ sq ft}} = 62 \text{ pounds of grass seed} \end{equation*}
62 pounds of grass seed at $3.50 each is $217.
\begin{equation*} 15500\text{ sq ft} \cdot \frac{1\text{ pound}}{350\text{ sq ft}} = 44.286 \xrightarrow{\text{round up}} 45 \text{ pounds of fertilizer} \end{equation*}
45 pounds of fertilizer at $1.25 each is $56.25. For labor, 4 hours at $45 per hour = $180. Total cost is $217+$56.25+$180=$453.25.
