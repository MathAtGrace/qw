Exercise 6.

Suppose you invest $200 per month for 10 years into an account earning 5% APR compounded monthly. You then leave the money, without making additional deposits, in the account for another 20 years.

  1. How much will you have after the first 10 years?

  2. How much will you have after the additional 20 years?

  3. How much total interest did you earn?

  4. What percent of the final balance is interest?
