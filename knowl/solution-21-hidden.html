To use a spreadsheet, we look at the formula solved for A, the future value. We enter

=4000*EXP(0.0275*7)

and get a result of $4849.11.

To use the formula, we have:

\(A=Pe^{rt}\)

\(P = 4000\text{,}\) amount invested

\(r = 0.0275\text{,}\) interest rate

\(t = 7\text{,}\) number of years

\begin{align*} A\amp=4000e^{(0.0275\cdot 7)}\\ \amp\approx\$4849.11 \end{align*}
