The trip is 193 miles each way, so \(193 \times 2 = 386\) miles roundtrip. Gas will cost
\begin{equation*} 386 \text{ miles} \cdot \frac{1\text{ gallon}}{40\text{ miles}} \cdot \frac{\$3.50}{1\text{ gallon}} = \$33.775 \approx \$33.78 \end{equation*}
After tax, the rental will cost $98.98 + 0.153($98.98) = $114.12 to the nearest cent. Thus, the total cost: $33.78 + $114.12 = $147.90
