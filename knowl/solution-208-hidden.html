  3. The range is:

    \begin{align*} Range \amp= Max - Min\\ \amp=29.4-15.2\\ \amp=14.2 \text{ seconds} \end{align*}

    The interquartile range (IQR) is:

    \begin{align*} IQR \amp= Q_{3} - Q_{1}\\ \amp=21.95-19.05\\ \amp=2.9 \text{ seconds} \end{align*}
